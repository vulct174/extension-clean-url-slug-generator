What is a URL slug?

A URL slug is the part that comes at the very end of a URL and is the exact address of a specific page on your website.

For example - in https://example.com/clean-url-slug, "clean-url-slug" is the slug.

Composing a short but descriptive slug for a URL of the web page can positively affect your page's SEO.
